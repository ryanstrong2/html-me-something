Makers

Modern society separates people by class. Within these classes there are many sublevels. From my point of view people in higher classes than mine treat the lower classes as if their value is also lower.

It seems many people are secure or comfortable in the class they are born into. Each person plays a role in the world economy. If there are not trash collectors, disease spreads. With out farmers there is no food. Many professions are paid the skill required not the importance to society.

If the wealth of the world was evenly distributed each person would earn about $40000 per year. $80,900,000,000,000 in currency around the world 7,400,000,000 people alive on Earth $10,900 per person

A redistribution like this would result in major resistance from everyone with greater wealth. I am surprised that most people have far less than this and do not resist the current imbalance. Also surprising is the hope of having a greater sum of money even with very low chance leads people to support this imbalance.
